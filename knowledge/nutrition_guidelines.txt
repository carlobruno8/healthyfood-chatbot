Source: World Health Organization (WHO)

A healthy diet includes:
- At least 400g (5 portions) of fruits and vegetables per day.
- Less than 10% of total energy intake from free sugars.
- Less than 30% of total energy intake from fats.
- Saturated fats should be replaced with unsaturated fats.
- Daily intake of dietary fiber through whole grains, legumes, fruits, and vegetables.

Source: European Food Safety Authority (EFSA)

- A varied and balanced diet supports adequate intake of essential nutrients.
- Regular consumption of whole grains contributes to fiber intake.
- Excessive intake of saturated fats and sugars is associated with increased health risks.
